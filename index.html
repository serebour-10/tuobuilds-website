Why TuoBuilds

Never Ignore These When Building

We guide our clients away from the most costly mistakes in Ghanaian construction — making sure your project is done the safe and smart way.

01

No BOQ (Bill of Quantities)

Starting without a cost breakdown is a trap. A proper BOQ saves you from unexpected expenses and overspending.

02

No Professional Supervision

A mason is not an engineer. Qualified site supervision protects you from structural defects and costly delays.

03

No Building Permit

Skipping permits can cost you fines or demolition. Always secure your legal approvals before you build.

04

GhIE Certified & Compliant

Our engineers operate under Ghana Institution of Engineers standards and international engineering codes.
